Po Ryan

I am a second year PhD student at Stanford University working with Prof. Gordon Wetzstein at the Stanford Computational Imaging Lab. My research lies in 3D content generation/reconstruction. My work is supported by the Stanford Graduate Fellowship.

Prior to Stanford, I spent my undergrad in Carnegie Mellon University working with Prof. Ioannis Gkioulekas.
